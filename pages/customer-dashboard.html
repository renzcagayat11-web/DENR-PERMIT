❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process my application?

Processing time varies by permit type. Most applications are processed within 5-15 business days.

What documents do I need to submit?

Required documents vary by permit type. Please refer to the specific permit requirements or contact our office.

How can I check my application status?

You can check your application status in the "My Applications" section of this dashboard.
