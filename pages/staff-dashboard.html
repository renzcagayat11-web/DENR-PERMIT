❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I approve an application?

Go to Applications, click "View" on an application, then click "Approve" in the modal.

What happens when I reject an application?

You must provide a reason for rejection. The applicant will be notified via email.

Can I change my decision after approving?

No, once approved or rejected, the decision cannot be changed. Contact admin for assistance.
