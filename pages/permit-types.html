Permit Types

Available Environmental Permits

Learn about the different types of environmental permits we offer and their requirements

📋

Permits

Official authorization for specific environmental activities

🌳

Community-Based Forest Management Agreement (CBFMA)

Forest management agreement for community-based sustainable forest utilization

Requirements:
  • Letter of Intent
  • SEC/DOLE/CDA Registration (for organization)
  • List of officers/members
  • Barangay clearance
  • Project proposal / management plan
⏱️ 15-20 working days 💰 Variable
⛓️

Permit to Import Chainsaw

Authorization to import chainsaws for legitimate forestry or agricultural use

Requirements:
  • Application form
  • Valid ID
  • Business permit (if company)
  • Justification for import
  • Invoice / details of chainsaw
⏱️ 5-10 working days 💰 ₱500.00
🪚

Permit to Purchase Chainsaw

Authorization to purchase chainsaws for legitimate forestry or agricultural activities

Requirements:
  • Application form
  • Valid ID
  • Barangay clearance
  • Reason for purchase
  • Proof of legal use (e.g., farming, business)
⏱️ 3-5 working days 💰 ₱300.00
🦌

Local Transport Permit (Wildlife)

Permit for transporting wildlife within the country for legitimate purposes

Requirements:
  • Application letter
  • Valid ID
  • Source of wildlife documents
  • Transport vehicle details
  • Destination permit (if required)
⏱️ 5-7 working days 💰 ₱200.00
🐾

Wildlife Farm Permit - Medium to Large Scale

Permit for operating wildlife farms with medium to large scale operations

Requirements:
  • Application form
  • Business registration
  • Environmental compliance certificate
  • Technical feasibility study
  • Facility layout plan
⏱️ 20-30 working days 💰 ₱1,000.00
🌿

Wildlife Farm Permit - Small Scale

Permit for operating small-scale wildlife farming operations

Requirements:
  • Application form
  • Valid ID
  • Barangay clearance
  • Site inspection report
  • Simple facility plan
⏱️ 10-15 working days 💰 ₱500.00
📜

Certificates

Official documents certifying compliance or registration

🪵

Certificate of Registration as Lumber Dealer

Official registration certificate for legitimate lumber dealing businesses

Requirements:
  • Application form
  • Business permit
  • DTI/SEC Registration
  • Valid ID
  • Storage facility inspection
⏱️ 7-10 working days 💰 ₱750.00
📦

Certificate of Registration as Importer of Lumber and Wood Materials

Registration certificate for importing lumber and wood materials

Requirements:
  • Application form
  • Import license
  • Business registration
  • Valid ID
  • Storage facility details
⏱️ 10-15 working days 💰 ₱1,500.00
🦜

Certificate of Wildlife Registration (CWR)

Registration certificate for legally owned wildlife specimens

Requirements:
  • Application form
  • Proof of legal acquisition
  • Valid ID
  • Wildlife inspection report
  • Facility inspection (if applicable)
⏱️ 5-7 working days 💰 ₱300.00
✅

Certifications

Documents validating compliance with environmental standards

🚚

Certification for the Transport of Non-Timber Forest Product Except Rattan

Certification for transporting non-timber forest products (excluding rattan)

Requirements:
  • Application letter
  • Valid ID
  • Source documentation
  • Transport details
  • Product inventory list
⏱️ 3-5 working days 💰 ₱150.00
🛡️

Clearances

Official clearances for specific activities or transport

🚐

Special Local Transport Permit (SLTP) (Wildlife)

Special permit for local transport of wildlife under specific conditions

Requirements:
  • Application letter
  • Valid ID
  • Wildlife ownership documents
  • Transport justification
  • Route plan and timeline
⏱️ 2-3 working days 💰 ₱100.00
